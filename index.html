The Cradle to Cradle Approach

Our team philosophy is to combine the dual goals of profit and sustainable development. We attain these goals by taking a holistic approach and following the fundamentals of “Cradle to Cradle” development.

This means designing projects that consider the entire lifecycle — from materials sourcing and construction methods to long-term environmental impact and community benefit.

Eco-Design

Meet the Team

Founded in 2023, Vista Frontier is led by two partners with deep experience in real estate development and international investing.

Michel Grondin

Co-Founder / Managing Partner

Michel Grondin has been personally investing, partnering with local developers, and developing land internationally for 10 years, with the primary focus on Mexico’s Riviera Maya. In 2023, along with Dan, Michel founded Vista Frontier to scale and pioneer the luxury sustainable market for high-end villas in Mexico’s growing Yucatán Peninsula.

Dan Berdelle

Co-Founder / Managing Partner

With a Master’s Degree in Real Estate Development from Colorado University – Boulder, Dan has successfully developed multiple land plots and single-family homes in the mountain town of Buena Vista, Colorado. These investments have gained 4x exit return over a four-year investment horizon for LPs.
