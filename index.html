The Cradle to Cradle Framework

We weave “Cradle to Cradle” thinking into every project we build. It shapes how we source materials, how we work with the land, and how we think about the decades of life a property will have long after we’ve handed it over — from its footprint on the environment to the communities it becomes part of.

Lower Basis

Hotel Melipona

Bacalar, Quintana Roo, Mexico

A boutique luxury hotel on the shores of Bacalar’s legendary Lagoon of Seven Colors — a full-service hospitality experience unlike anything in the region.

  • Private villa accommodations with dedicated concierge service and hotel-caliber polish.
  • Farm-to-table dining, spa treatments, and curated excursions across the lagoon.
  • Sustainably designed to blend seamlessly into Bacalar’s pristine natural landscape.
  • Artisan craftsmanship paired with modern luxury finishes in every villa.

Hotel Melipona — Phase 2

Laguna Bacalar, Quintana Roo, Mexico

An intimate extension of Hotel Melipona — a cluster of small private bungalows along the shores of Laguna Bacalar.

  • Secluded bungalows tucked into the jungle canopy, designed as private retreats.
  • Elevated timber walkways connecting each unit to a central palapa gathering space.
  • Locally sourced materials and shingled pyramid roofs, oriented toward the lagoon.
  • Design-forward extension of Melipona’s hospitality program, deepening the guest offering.
  • Minimal site disturbance — layout preserves existing landscape and canopy.

Leadership

The Managing Partners

Vista Frontier is led by two principals with complementary expertise in international real estate development, investment management, and sustainable design.

Michel Grondin

Co-Founder & Managing Partner

Michel brings over a decade of hands-on experience investing, partnering with local developers, and developing land across international markets, with a primary focus on Mexico’s Riviera Maya. His deep on-the-ground network and market knowledge drive Vista Frontier’s sourcing advantage and local execution capability.

Dan Berdelle

Co-Founder & Managing Partner

Dan holds a Master’s Degree in Real Estate Development from the University of Colorado – Boulder. He brings over 10 years of experience across real estate development, including roles with a private investment group and a publicly traded homebuilder. As a founder and principal, he focuses on disciplined execution, project management, and delivering high-quality, hospitality-driven environments.
